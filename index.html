A Little More About Me

I use Adobe Creative Suite to craft bold visuals that tell a story—whether it’s a poster, motion graphic, or social media campaign. Figma helps me prototype sleek, user-friendly designs and collaborate in real time, while Notion keeps my projects organized and my creative chaos in check. For data storytelling, I lean on Tableau and Power BI to turn complex datasets into intuitive dashboards that reveal patterns, drive decisions, and impress stakeholders. And when I need to dig into the details, Excel is my go-to for everything from quick calculations to building financial models that keep projects on track and under budget. Each tool plays a role in helping me bridge the gap between design, data, and impact.
