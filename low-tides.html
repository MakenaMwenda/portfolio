Project Description

As part of a simulated analytics project inspired by the Royal Bank of Canada, I worked with dummy data to analyze client engagement with financial products using structured SQL queries. The goal was to demonstrate how data insights could support business teams in identifying high-performing product types and upsell opportunities based on client demographics and product usage.
